AI in Banking - Key Insights

Fraud Detection Patterns

  • Transaction Amount: Higher amounts show increased fraud probability
  • Time Patterns: Certain hours show higher fraud rates
  • Location Risk: Transactions from high-risk locations flagged more often
  • Behavioral Changes: Deviations from normal patterns trigger alerts

Loan Approval Factors

  • Credit Score: Primary factor in approval decisions
  • Income Level: Direct correlation with approval likelihood
  • Debt-to-Income: Lower ratios increase approval chances
  • Employment History: Stability contributes to positive decisions
